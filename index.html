Stay & Fly â€“
2nd Place Master Project at ESME

Stay & Fly is a full-stack Web3 travel platform designed to reshape the way people book, manage, and resell their travel plans. Developed by three final-year engineering students from ESME â€“ Nathan Verdeyme, Colin Verriere, and Nabil Kerkoub â€“ the project was realized in partnership with Amadeus, a global leader in travel technology.

The platform combines hotel booking, flight reservation, secondary market resale, and activity planning in a single interface. It leverages modern technologies such as ReactJS for the front-end, Node.js/Express for the backend, and Web3.js to interact with a private Ethereum blockchain hosted on Microsoft Azure.

Main features:

  • Hotel reservation & resale: Users can book hotels using the Amadeus API. In case of a change of plans, they can resell their reservation as an NFT on a private marketplace. Each reservation is tokenized using a custom ERC-721 smart contract, ensuring secure, traceable transactions via blockchain. Hotels benefit from an exclusive preview period to repurchase reservations before they're publicly listed.
  • Flight price negotiation: The platform introduces a dynamic negotiation system where users can propose a price for a flight ticket. Partner airlines can accept, reject, or counter-offer using an admin interface. This model promotes better seat occupancy and personalized pricing strategies.
  • Activity planner: Integrated with the Ticketmaster API and Google Maps, this module allows users to search for concerts, sports events, and local attractions at their destination. Users can build a personalized itinerary and receive a full summary by email.

Technical highlights:

  • Dual-server architecture (Node.js): one for hotel/activities/blockchain, another for flights/negotiation
  • JWT authentication, secure payments, and smart contract integration via Web3.js
  • Azure SQL database with relational schemas for users, reservations, transactions, and flight negotiations
  • React components designed for modularity, responsiveness, and smooth UX
  • Smart contracts (ReservationNFT & Marketplace) deployed via Remix and controlled via MetaMask

The project was rigorously tested with both manual (Postman, SoapUI) and automated approaches, ensuring stability, scalability, and a professional-grade product. A private Geth-based Ethereum blockchain ensures full ownership and transparency of hotel bookings through NFT technology.

Stay & Fly is targeted at casual travelers, professionals, and agencies who seek flexibility, transparency, and innovation in their travel planning. With its blend of blockchain, AI-based pricing models, and real-time API integrations, Stay & Fly represents a new era in travel tech.
